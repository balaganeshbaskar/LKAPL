Sheets

For sanding applications by hand or specific machine Norton sheets and strips are best suited. Especially for complicated finishes that are used in wood working, in the paint and clear coat business, in solid surface material sanding and in many other metallurgical testing and finishing situations.


Paper Sheet

For complex, detailed, and intricate jobs requiring specific finishes, like producing and restoring wall sanding and wooden furniture. Norton offers a range of paper sheets that ensure consistent cut-rate and long life. Due to their open structure and non-clogging properties, these paper sheets are ideal for wall sanding, paint stripping, automotive sanding, and finishing.

Grit range - 50 -200


No-Fill Sheet

Non-loading Paper with latex-reinforced backing for less clogging. Known for their fine cut rates and sharp abrasive grains, Non-loading papers are ideal for wet and applications. Best suited for wood sealer /PU/ Polyester Sanding and automotive sanding.

Grit Range - 150-600


waterproof Sheet

Norton's best-in-class latex yellow waterproof paper comes with premium abrasive grains to provide a high cut rate and smooth finish. Ideal for both wet and dry sanding applications. Best suited for wall putty sanding, auto paint stripping, and wood polishing.

Grit range: 60 - 1000


Cloth Sheet

Cloth sheets are engineered for maximum flexing and durability. Ideal for removal of light burr and polishing metal surfaces
